Accomplishments

View RIT's victories over the years!

  • 2012

    RIT wins NPPL Huntington Beach

    In one of RIT's greatest events of all time, A-line proceeds to win in the finals during a one on one, B-Line takes third, and C-line takes 4th.

  • October 2012

    RIT wins NCPA - Brick City Open

    RIT wins the finals against University at Buffalo Bulls two out of three matches.

  • October 2013

    RIT wins NCPA - NEIC Event #2

    RIT struggled to get out of the preliminaries, but ended up moving all the way to finals beating Drexel University.

  • February 2014

    RIT wins NCPA - NEIC Event #3

    RIT wins against Suny Oswego.

  • October 2016

    RIT wins NCPA - NEIC AA Conference #1

    RIT wins the 1st event of the year against Drexel University.
